Physical Application Requirements

Applicants are required to submit their documents physically at the Nairobi City County offices. Please ensure you include the following:

  • Recommendation Letter from your institution
  • Certificate of Good Conduct
  • Application Letter addressed to the County Secretary
  • Updated Curriculum Vitae (CV)

Ensure all documents are neatly arranged and properly labeled before submission.
